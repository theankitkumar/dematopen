    To work in the stock market, it is very important to have a Demat account according to the rules of SEBI.<\/p>

    What exactly is a Demat Account?<\/span><\/strong><\/h3>

    To buy and hold stocks in the stock market, you need a demat account, just like you need a savings account at a bank to keep your funds safe.<\/p>

    How to select a Demat Account?<\/span><\/strong><\/h3>

    Demat account can be selected based on the trustworthiness of the brokerage firm, minimum brokerage charges and annual maintenance charges.<\/p>
