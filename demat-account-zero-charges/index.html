What is a Demat Account?

Think of it as your digital wallet for stocks! Just like you store money in a bank account, a Demat account stores your shares and securities digitally - safe, secure, and easily accessible.

Simple Definition

A Demat Account (Dematerialized Account) is like a digital locker where all your shares, bonds, mutual funds, and other investments are stored electronically. No more physical share certificates - everything is digital, safe, and instantly accessible!

Why Do You Need It?

In India, it's mandatory to have a Demat account to buy or sell shares in the stock market. It's like having an Aadhaar card for investing - you simply can't trade without it!
